Alcyone Frontlines
---
The game has 2 teams. Each team has 1 RTS player and 5 Soldier/hero players. 
The RTS player plays Starcraft as normal, while the 5 other players is able to pick a soldier depending on the race that the RTS player picked.

The map has 3 lanes, waves will spawn on each side until their spawner for the respective lane is destroyed. 
The RTS player needs provide his Soldiers with healers in order for soldiers to be able to heal. 

Killing hostile units will provide the RTS soldier with Minerals and sometimes gas,
If a player is killed by an enemy soldier or by the RTS player themselves, then the RTS player will receive 500 Mineral + 300 Gas.

---
The map is named "Alcyone Frontlines_Alpha" and can be found on the European Servers.
